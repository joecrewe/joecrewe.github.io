About Me

I’m Joe Crewe, an offline editor based in Scotland with 20 years of experience delivering factual, entertainment, and branded content for major UK broadcasters and corporate clients. I cut with rhythm, speed and precision — and I’m equally confident working solo or in collaboration with producers and execs. Clients include BBC Studios, Channel 5, Channel 4, Red Bull, Shell, and many more.
